About Us

The 2021 JMPSC team consists of both adults and students working together with the common goal of fostering a passion for math within students. These dedicated individuals are in no way short of their own accomplishments, ranging from APMO qualifiers, AMC 10 perfect scorers, JMO qualifiers, Mathcounts nationals qualifiers, and people also on the MAA problem proposal committee.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't make it to the competition?

If you are unable to attend the competition's Zoom meeting, you will be ineligble for prizes. However, we will release the competition problems and answers after the test is over so you can try solving it on your own then.

Do I need to have my camera/microphone on during the test?

You may have your camera on during the competition if you wish. Having your camera on during the competition is not mandatory. Your microphone should be off during the duration of the test. If any JMPSC staff sees inappropriate behavior, we will remove you from the meeting and you will be disqualified.
