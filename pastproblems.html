2021 JMPSC Problems

Sprint Round:


Contestants are given 50 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 3 points, for a total of 60 points.

Accuracy Round:


Contestants are given 60 minutes to solve 15 questions, each question is worth 4 points, for a total of 60 points.

Invitational Round:


The combined score of the sprint and accuracy rounds determine whether or not the contestant is qualified for the
invitational round. Qualifiers are at least the top 15% of scorers. Contestants are given 80 minutes to solve 15
questions, each question is worth 15 points, for a total of 225 points. Final placements and prize winners are decided
first by scores, then by the speed of submission.
