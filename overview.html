General Information





Eligibility



To be eligible for prizes, you must have been in middle school or below in the 2020–2021 school year. If you are older, you may participate, but will not receive prizes should you win.

Date



The sprint and accuracy rounds will be held from 10:00 AM - 12:20 PM PST on July 10th. Qualifiers will be invited to the invitaitonal round held from 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM PST on July 11th. Please take a look at the FAQ section if you cannot make it to the test.

Platform



JMPSC will be hosted on a Zoom session accessible only through the learning platform schoolhouse.world (making an account will be necessary). Further instruction will be send out once you sign up through this google form.

Logistics





Prizes



To be eligible for prizes, you must have been in middle school or below in the 2020–2021 school year. If you are older, you may participate, but will not receive prizes should you win.

Thanks to our generous sponsors, we will be able to hand out $2026 worth of prizes to each of:

  • Gold: Top-10 finishers overall (based on scores from all three rounds)

  • Silver: Top-10 finishers in accuracy who did not rank in Gold

  • Bronze: Top-10 finishers in sprint who did not rank Gold or Silver

    This means that regardless or whether you qualify for invitationals, you have a chance! Note taht through this system, every participant is limited to winning one prize.

    Prizes will not be publicized due to email complaints on prize selection, but to give you a taste for what you can win, first place gold wins $150 and second place gold wins $100, in the form of any gift card you want.

    • Rounds



  • Sprint Round: Contestants are given 50 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 3 points, for a total of 60 points.

  • Accuracy Round: Contestants are given 60 minutes to solve 15 questions, each question is worth 4 points, for a total of 60 points.

  • Invitational Round: The combined score of the sprint and accuracy rounds determine whether or not the contestant is qualified for the invitaitonal round. Qualifiers are at least the top 15% of scorers. Contestants are given 80 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 15 points, for a total of 300 points.

  • Final placements and prize winers are decided first by scores, then by the speed of submission.
