General Information





Eligibility and Date



  • To be eligble for prizes, you must be under the age of 16. Keep in mind that this test is geared towards middle school students. If you are older, you may participate, but you will not receive a prize should you win.

  • The contest date is July 10th. Please take a look at the FAQ section if you cannot make it to the test.

    • Rounds:



  • Sprint Round: Contestants are given 50 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 3 points, for a total of 60 points.

  • Accuracy Round: Contestants are given 60 minutes to solve 15 questions, each question is worth 4 points, for a total of 60 points.

  • Invitational Round: The combined score of the sprint and accuracy rounds determine whether or not the contestant is qualified for the invitaitonal round. Contestants are given 80 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 15 points, for a total of 300 points.
